About Us

We're all related (literally): Karen & Alex are siblings and Andrew is their cousin. We met Charles via working at a startup.

We. LOVE. Hackathons. Why? You get to: 1) build something new, 2) excite an audience, 3) meet great people. In October 2014, we built a prototype of Proximity for the Salesforce $1M Hackathon and won 1st Place, taking home $100k.

Bridging offline to online experiences are what we are experts in. Whether its within the realm of e-commerce or medical devices, we're excited to bridget the gap in communication. Armed with the development and startup experiences we've all gained at top universities and major tech companies, we're ready and motivated to bring Proximity to the public.
